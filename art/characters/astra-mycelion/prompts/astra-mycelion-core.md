# Prompt Base: Astra Mycelion

Highly original 2D action platformer protagonist sprite art. Astra Mycelion is a bio-cyber guardian made from translucent obsidian glass, living mycelium circuitry and floating ceramic armor shards. Asymmetric dark triangular prism head with one vertical amber slit-lens, no eyes, no horns, no white mask. Three small floating halo shards around the head. Compact athletic body, luminous amber core suspended in ribbed graphite torso, burgundy fungal fiber scarf, floating shoulder plates, thin segmented legs ending in blade-like hover skates, filament whip-lance arm and shield emitter disk arm. Palette: obsidian black, graphite gray, bone ceramic accents, burgundy fungal fibers, amber/gold energy and small violet highlights. Avoid any resemblance to Hollow Knight, Hornet, Mega Man, Mega Man X or blue robot armor.
